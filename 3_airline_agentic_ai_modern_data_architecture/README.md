# Agentic AI with Modern Enterprise Data Architecture for Airline Companies

## Book Overview
This book explores the transformative potential of agentic artificial intelligence systems within modernized enterprise data architectures specifically tailored for airline companies. As the aviation industry faces unprecedented challenges and opportunities in the digital age, this comprehensive guide provides practical frameworks, strategies, and implementation roadmaps for leveraging autonomous AI agents to revolutionize airline operations, customer experience, and business models.

## Target Audience
- Airline industry executives and decision-makers
- Data architects and enterprise architects in the aviation sector
- IT leaders and technology strategists in airline companies
- Data scientists and AI specialists working with airline data
- Business analysts focused on aviation industry transformation

## Chapters
1. **Introduction to Agentic AI in Aviation**
2. **Modern Enterprise Data Architecture Fundamentals**
3. **Agentic AI Systems: Principles and Applications**
4. **Aviation Data Landscape and Challenges**
5. **Data Fabric and Data Mesh for Airlines**
6. **Autonomous AI Agents in Airline Operations**
7. **Customer Experience Transformation with Agentic AI**
8. **Implementation Strategy and Roadmap**
9. **Governance, Ethics, and Regulatory Considerations**
10. **Future Trends and Innovation Opportunities**

## How to Use This Book
Each chapter builds on concepts from previous chapters while offering practical insights that can be applied independently. Case studies, diagrams, and implementation frameworks are provided to help readers visualize and execute the concepts presented.
